Rav Shimon Frankfort

1642-1712

Biography

Rav Shimon ben Rav Yisrael was born in Mezeritz (Poland). In 1655 he fled from Schwerin (then Poland, nowadays Germany) to Amsterdam He served as a dayan in the beis din hagodol of Amsterdam and as head of the chevre kadishe and was a teacher of Mishne and Gemore for more than forty years.

Major Works

In 1703 he published a sefer on haloche Dinei Semachos, which was later on published by his son Moshe under the name Sefer Hachaim, translated into several languages, including English (The Book of Life).
