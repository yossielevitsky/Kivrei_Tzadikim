About this Project

Our Mission

The "Rabbis of Amsterdam" project aims to preserve and present the rich history of rabbinical leadership in one of Europe's most significant Jewish centers. From the Golden Age to the modern era, Amsterdam has been home to scholars, mystics, and leaders who steered their communities through times of prosperity and adversity.

This website serves as a digital archive and educational resource, offering accessible biographies and historical context for students, historians, and the general public.
